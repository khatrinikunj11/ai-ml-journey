2. Go to Bank
3. Sleep

ranking hard to easy
on hardest game winning - he gets 100% of his bid + his bid 
on easiest game winning - he gets 10% of his bid + his bid
middle games winning - he gets 20% to 90% considering the game + his bid
if he choses 11 option that is a side quest
Casino offers player a deal - 
If player won atleast 7 games out of 10 - Casino takes a 1000$ Loan for him without any interest
If player cant win atleast 7 games out of 10 - Casino will take 1000$ Loan and takes 900$ from it leaving player with 100$ and the whole interest of the loan.

### if he chooses (2):

if he says yes

1.200$

4.800$

### if he choses (3):
